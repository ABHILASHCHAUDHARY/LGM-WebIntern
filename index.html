Welcome To CARE WELL INDIA TRUST

We are a grass-root Indian NGO working for the empowerment of street-connected Senior Citizens. We believe that Senior Citizens are not on the street by choice but because they are left with no other alternative.

OUR VISION

“Our vision is to build a sustainable, human friendly & environment friendly where we could achieve their fullest potential” Our vision focuses to build an environment at a certain level or rate where they feel safe, show hospitable and give them pleasant environment where they can showcase their talents up to full potential.

OUR MISSION

“We are committed to work with communities to bridge the gap between accessibility and resources to enhance the social equalities” Our mission focuses to build an environment where the children use their full potential to achieve their dreams. The sustainable development ‘s first goal (SDG) is to alleviate poverty which means “to end poverty in all its forms, everywhere”. We can achieve this aim by providing education, by providing equal rights to economic resources, access to basic services, poverty and land control, natural resources and new technologies.

OUR VALUE

“We strongly believe in Equality, Integrity, Care, Ethics, and Sustainability” Our organization focuses on health, children education, women empowerment, skills development, environment protection and disaster response with belief in equality, being hospitable to the one in need, work towards development being eco-friendly, following ethics required, and provide guardianship.
